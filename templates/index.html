"Welcome to Share A Meal"

"Welcome to Share a Meal, a community-driven platform dedicated to reducing food waste and feeding those in need. Whether you have surplus food to donate, need assistance with meals, or want to volunteer as a delivery partner, we connect you with opportunities to make a meaningful impact. Together, we can ensure that no meal goes to waste and that every person has access to nourishing food."

Become A Donor

"Do you have extra food? Share it with those in need by donating through our platform. We work closely with local NGOs and food banks to ensure your contributions reach those who need them most."

Become A Reciever

"If you or someone you know is struggling to put food on the table, we're here to help. Request a meal from nearby donors or food banks quickly and confidentially through our platform. so lets come together."

Steps

01

Create an account by providing your name, email, and password, or login if you already have an account.



02

Decide whether to donate, recieve, or deliver food. Provide neccessary details, and confirm to proceed.



03

Track the status of actions and mark tasks as compeleted once the donations is received or the delivery is made.



04

You've made a positive impact by sharing or receiving a meal. Thank you for contributing to community!

World Food Day

Join us as we come together to celebrate World Food Day, a global initiative to raise awareness and take action against hunger and food insecurity. This year’s theme, “Water is Life, Water is Food: Leave No One Behind,” highlights the vital role of water in ensuring sustainable food systems for all.

📅 Date: October 16, 2025
⏰ Time: 3:00 PM – 8:00 PM
📍 Location: Central Park, New York City

Let’s work together to create a world where everyone has access to nutritious food and no one is left behind. Bring your friends and family to make this day a meaningful celebration of food, sustainability, and community.

Zero Descrimination Day

Join us as we observe Zero Discrimination Day, a global movement promoting equality, inclusion, and the right for everyone to live with dignity—regardless of race, gender, nationality, disability, or any other status. This year’s theme, “Embracing Diversity, Ending Discrimination,” calls for collective action to create a world free from prejudice and injustice.

📅 Date: March 1, 2025
⏰ Time: 3:00 PM – 8:00 PM
📍 Location: Federation Square, Melbourne

Let’s stand together for equality, respect, and human rights for all. Bring your voice, your stories, and your support to make this day a powerful statement against discrimination.

International Day of Awareness of Food Loss and Waste

oin us in observing the International Day of Food Loss and Waste Awareness, a global initiative to highlight the importance of reducing food waste and creating sustainable food systems. Every year, millions of tons of food are lost or wasted, impacting food security, economies, and the environment. This year’s theme, “Reducing Food Waste: A Shared Responsibility”, encourages individuals, businesses, and communities to take action.

📅 Date: September 29, 2025
⏰ Time: 2:00 PM – 7:00 PM
📍 Location: Trafalgar Square, London

Let's work together to build a future where food is valued, resources are conserved, and waste is minimized. Bring your friends and family to be part of the change!
