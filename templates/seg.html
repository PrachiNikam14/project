Guide to Waste Segregation

Categories of Waste

Organic Waste

Used for biodegradable waste like food scraps and garden waste, ideal for composting.

Plastic Waste

Designed for dry recyclable waste such as paper, plastic, and glass.

Medical Waste

Used for medical and sanitary waste including bandages and syringes.

Hazardous Waste

Meant for hazardous or metallic waste like cans, tins, and e-waste.

Non-Recyclable Waste

For non-recyclable and soiled waste like used tissues or mixed garbage.
